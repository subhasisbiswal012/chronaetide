Memory I

In that quiet room we met, our first close embrace and a shy, lingering kiss that finally said what words could not. We walked away with each other's scent on our clothes and a new, breathless language between us.

Memory II

In the park we met again, your kisses along my neck left me breathless and quietly wanting more. We smiled for the photo while you held me tight; I wished then to kiss you back and hold you closer than I dared.

Memory III

Back where we first met, I teased your hair and let our hands wander into shy, lingering kisses. A playful little fight and bright laughter a closeness that left us smiling and wanting more.

Memory IV

On my birthday, you became everything — the plan, the place, the people, the reason the day felt alive. At sunset above it all, I wanted to hold you closer, kiss you longer, and thank you for loving me the way only you do.

Memory V

Our first intimate moment — where I wanted you beside me a little longer, wrapped in quiet closeness. I wished to hold you, to cuddle deeper, and linger in the warmth we had just discovered together.

Memory VI

We wandered the mall, shared a meal, laughed endlessly, letting the day feel light and full. When it was time to part, I wanted one more hug, one more moment, wishing time would slow and never let us end the day.
